/start - Begin conversation with bot

/help - Print help for commands

/set - Add new image variable.
Firstly send variable name and then picture to assign.
Remember that picture should contain face in the middle of it for correct work.

/eval - Evaluate expression.
Use variable names or even numbers and mathematical operations, such as + or - to find something new.
For example, you can upload pictures of man with and without glasses, find their difference and add result to new picture.

/from - Calculate images between 2 samples.
Usage: /from image1 image2 steps
image1 and image2 - your variables (as in /eval function), steps - number of images to generate

/cancel - Cancel adding variable
